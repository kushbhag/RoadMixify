Welcome to Road Mixify!

To get started, login to your spotify account

Your Personalized Playlist Generator

Let's get started on creating the perfect playlist for your next road trip, workout, or event.

Set Duration

To start making your playlist, select any duration that is less than 24 hours.

Select Artists and Albums

Next, personalize your playlist by selecting any number of artists or albums.

Advanced Settings

You can choose to make the playlist private, or prevent any explicit songs. You can also selectively remove any artist's album that you don't want on the playlist.

Add Playlist

If you like the playlist, click add and you're done! Otherwise feel free to refresh the playlist, or go back and change some settings.

Get Listening!

The playlist has automatically been added to your Spotify account. A link will also be displayed to take you directly to it!
